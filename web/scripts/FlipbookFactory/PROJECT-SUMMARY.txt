HOW IT WORKS:
-------------
1. Define workflow in YAML:
   
   workflow:
     - step: download_video
       id: download
       params: {url: "https://youtube.com/..."}
   
     - step: extract_frames
       params:
         input: ${download.output}  â† Reference previous step
